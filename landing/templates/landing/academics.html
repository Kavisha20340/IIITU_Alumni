Departments

School of Computing

  • Vision

    • To provide excellent technical education in computer science and engineering and produce competent engineers and professionals with high ethical values prepared for life long learning.

  • Mission

    • - To impart quality and value based education in computer science and engineering to solve real world problems with an inclination towards societal issues and research.
    - Nurturing the students of today into resolute builders of the future
    - To produce computer professionals who can face not only the present but also future challenges of I.T. Industry

School of Electronics

  • Vision

    • To provide excellent technical education in electronics engineering and produce future professionals, to prepare student for professional career with continuous learning.

  • Mission

    • - To inculcate harmonious and respectful environment.
    - Develop a culture of group responsibility for mentoring ECE students.
    - To produce electronics engineers who have a passion to respond to rapidly changing social and economic environment.

School of Basic Sciences

  • Vision

    • To lay sound foundation in basic sciences and prepare the students to learn engineering better.

  • Mission

    • - Acquire abilities to evaluate problems using Mathematics.
    - Provide a platform to keep abreast with current developments in Science.
    - Imbibe communication skills and inculcate human values.
